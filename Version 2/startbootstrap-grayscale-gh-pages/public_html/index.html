Smoothie Bowls.

Real food, for the body and the mind.

Get Yours Today!

Smoothie Bowls are the bread and butter here at Berry Smooth. We believe that having a good tasting product isn't enough anymore. We want to make good tasting Smoothie Bowls that are Organic, low calorie, and accessible! Along with making healthy products that taste good, we also want them to look good. We take Strawberries, Bananas, Coconut, and any requested fruits/vegetables and arrange them in a way that looks and tastes great!

Smoothies.

Simply Tasty, Simply Better.

Try One Today!

Smoothies are one of the most accessible items around! We took this concept of the Smoothie and redeveloped it from the ground up! If you look closely at the packaging of a Smoothie, you will quickly see that good tasting Smoothies contain a lot of sugar. Because our vision is "healthy drinks for all" we make Smoothies with 0 added sugar. Instead of added sugar, we blend a mixture of fruits and vegetables to compensate for the bland taste.
