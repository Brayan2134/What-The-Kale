About Us.

We at Berry Smooth are revolutionizing the way we drink smoothies and smoothiebowls! We believe that you can have something that tastes good, AND is healthy! We blend a mix of rich, creamy, and flavorful ingredients, so you can have a unique taste like you've never experienced before! Not just that, but we know that in order to live a happy life you need to have a healthy life. We don't add any artificial chemicals, sweeteners, or anything that is harmful for us, rather sweet and natural ingredients that have the nutrients we need, and the taste we want! With only Organic and Local Produce (if possible) you can be happy that what you're consuming here comes from here! Want to know what's also going to be happy? Your wallet! We know that eating healthy can be expensive, so by buying our fruits and vegetables in bulk, we can cut down costs and transfer those savings for you! We don't believe that certain people should only be able to eat healthy, so we do everything in our power to bring good, nutritious and organic smoothies/smoothiebowls to EVERYONE!
