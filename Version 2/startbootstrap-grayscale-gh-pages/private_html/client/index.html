Privacy Policy

Data Protection.

We know that information is private. We at Berry Smooth agree with that, and believe that it should remain that way! Because of this, we took steps to protect and discard your data. We've split this modal into sections, so you can see how your data is used and how we manipulate the data.

1) Input.

In order for us to receive data, we don't actively search for data, like other companies would. Since we don't collect data, we need for you to input more information than what you're used to. Don't worry, we get that some of the information is really personal, and you don't want that to be shared. Because of this, we made some of the fields optional, so you can feel safe giving out only the information you want. There is a downside of us having less information. If one of the methods of contact doesn't work, then there isn't another way to contact you and we can't really hire you.

2) Sending The Information.

When you click the send button, you should be redirected to another page confirming the submission. In some known cases, overload of the application process, may send the information without confirmation. This does not mean that we're keylogging, rather that the page that you should go to after pushing the send button is overloaded. This does mean that we still received your information, but you just don't know. When you send the information, you are sending it through AWS (Amazon Web Services). Because of it's API's which we have used, the data is secured with the newest services available!

3) Manipulation of Data.

This is the scary part for most people, when they send the information - what we do with it. Don't worry with us, as we just use the data as records! The first thing we get is an email telling us that a submission has been sent to us. We look at our database and verify that the submission has passed through. Once verification is complete, we then make sure to look at your information (which you provided) and setup an interview! We're just confirming that the data matches what we want.

4) Discarding Data.

Once the interview process is over, we check the data provided to us again and then make a decision if we believe your a good candidate for the position. If all positions are filled, we simply just go to the database, and click the delete button on the database. We then turn off the database to prevent online attacks, and return to normal operations.

Summary.

- Provide data
- Use data to assess candidates
- interview
- Delete data
