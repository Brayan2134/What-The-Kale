What's an Açaí Bowl?

A Açaí Bowl is a thicker version of a smoothie. It's thicker, creamier, and packed with more flavor! With Açaí, a South American palm tree producing small edible blackish-purple berries, and sweet flavors like bananas and strawberries, you'll be left craving for more!
